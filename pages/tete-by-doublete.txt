Key Aspects of Limited Series Toy Production

Design & Development: Focus on skill-building, such as STEM concepts, cognitive skills, or motor skills.

Material Selection: Sustainable options like wood or recycled materials are preferred.

Production Method: Small-run, handmade, or utilizing quick-turnaround methods like laser cutting to reduce costs while allowing for customization.

Packaging Design: Interactive packaging that can be used for storage or as part of the play experience, with clear safety information and educational value.

Compliance: Ensuring all materials and designs meet safety standards for their target age group.
