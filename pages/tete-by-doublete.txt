Handmade educational play products.

We design each product, laser-cut it from wood, hand-paint it, and pair it with interactive, eco-friendly packaging made from recycled materials.




Key Aspects of Limited-Series Toy Production

Design & Development: Focused on building STEM understanding, cognitive development, and motor skills.

Material Selection: Preference for sustainable options such as wood and recycled materials.

Production Method: Small-run, handmade production supported by quick-turnaround methods like laser cutting, allowing customization while keeping costs under control.

Packaging Design: Interactive packaging that supports storage and can become part of the play experience, with clear safety information and educational value.

Compliance: All materials and designs meet safety standards for the intended age group.
